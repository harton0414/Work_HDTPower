ABOUT HDT

MAZO team has more than ten years of experience in the management, especially specializing in the development of magnetic material and magnetic inductors, the ability of professional design and application of AC reactors and equipment development/ mold simulation design.

Today’s magnetic components are already high-current, light and thin, stable and large scale quality and the need for flexibility. At present, the company introduces updated automation equipment factory to produce products, also has a good R&D design and transformation capabilities. Make product manufacturing more competitive, diversity and stability are designed to provide the best service to meet customers’ demand.

Today, HDT headquarter is established in Taipei, Taiwan, HDT branch was established separately in Yangmei District, Hsinchu HDT Power, Kunchan District East China HDT Power, Dalang District, Donnguan, South China HDT Power. Customers who cooperate with each base to effectively operate fast-service cooperation and support transactions at various locations. We cherish every opportunity to cooperate with our customers and constantly strive for excellence, self-improvement introduces stable new products to meet the needs of more meta-products and hope to get more customers’ recognition and affirmation.

In the future, facing the new competitive landscape of the new global electronic market, will continue to follow “to meet and exceed customer expectations, continuous research and development, innovative products” the concept of enterprise development, with an optimized management system, rapid development capability, excellent product quality and perfect service. Bravely meet new challenges to pursue new high front, strive to become expert in the field of electronic components and technology solutions, an international company with technological leadership and core competitive advantages.
